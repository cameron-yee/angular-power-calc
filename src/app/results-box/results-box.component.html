The purpose of this calculator is to provide intervention researchers in science education with an empirically-based intraclass and covariate correlation for use in a priori power analyses. The values provided by this calculator were generated using multilevel models applied to K-12 science achievement outcome data from Michigan, Texas, and Wisconsin (see Spybrook, Westine, & Taylor, 2016).
